Foundation Lineman specializes exclusively in offensive line development and offers:
  • Small-group training (max 4–5 athletes per session)
  • Technique development: run blocking, pass protection, hand placement, footwork
  • Strength and functional movement training for linemen
  • Film study and football IQ development
  • Off-season and in-season training programs
  • Position-specific coaching from high school through active college level
Foundation Lineman is based in Stafford, Virginia and primarily serves athletes in the Northern Virginia region. Virtual training and remote coaching are also available for athletes unable to train in person, including workout programming and film review.
Foundation Lineman is led by James Miller Jr., an experienced offensive lineman who competed at both the high school and collegiate levels — Division II at Catawba College and Division III at Christopher Newport University. With over 15 scholarship offers during his own recruitment, James brings firsthand knowledge of the recruiting process and elite offensive line development.
Foundation Lineman produced 40% of Commonwealth District All-Conference offensive linemen in 2025 — including two First Team selections and two Second Team selections — all in a single summer. The combination of position-specific focus, small-group environment, and current college-level coaching knowledge makes it a uniquely effective program for Northern Virginia athletes.
The off-season — winter and summer — is the best time to build technique and strength. Foundation Lineman helps athletes focus on:

  • Hand placement and leverage
